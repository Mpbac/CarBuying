Car Buying Study

Are you buying a car within the next month?

We are researchers at Dartmouth College collecting data on car purchases and financing, for an academic study.

We need data for our study, and upon survey completion will pay you:

  • $5 for taking a 3 minute survey today
  • $20 for sending us readable pictures with the details of any car purchase/financing transaction you make, if you do end up buying a car in the next month.

Please complete this initial screening survey to see if you are eligible to participate in this study!

Your responses will be kept confidential and used for research purposes only.
